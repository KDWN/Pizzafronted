How to add items to cart

Click on the home page image at the top left.
Look at the menu section.
Click on the box containing the image of the pizza you want to order.
Enter how many of that pizza you want in the box.
If you don't want to order that pizza press cancel, input nothing, or input 0.
Please note that we cannot sell more than 100 of a single pizza type in one order.
You are able to reduce the amount of a single type of pizzas by inputting a negative nuber, however we can't sell you negative pizzas.

How to complete purchase

Click on the checkout button at the top.
Press the complete purchase button to purchase everything in your cart. (Demo website so nothing will be purchased)
Click on the minus and plus buttons to reduce and increase the amount of a pizza type you want to order.
Click on the bin image and then click ok on the newly opened menu to remove a pizza type from your order.
Click on the clear cart button to empty your cart.
